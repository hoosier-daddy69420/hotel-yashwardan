About Hotel Yashwardhan

Hotel Yashwardhan is a luxurious 4-star hotel located in the heart of the city. We have been serving guests for over 25 years, and have a reputation for excellence in service and comfort. Our hotel features comfortable rooms, a restaurant, a fitness center, and a conference center. We pride ourselves on providing the best in comfort and convenience for our guests.

Location

Hotel Yashwardhan is located in the downtown area of the city, within walking distance of popular attractions, restaurants, and shops. The airport is just a short drive away.

History

Hotel Yashwardhan was founded in 2022 by Mr. Yashwardhan Dhiman. Since then, we have been committed to providing the best in comfort and convenience for our guests. We have consistently received high ratings and accolades for our service and amenities, and are proud to be one of the premier hotels in the city.

Our Team

Our team at Hotel Yashwardhan is dedicated to making your stay with us as comfortable and enjoyable as possible. From the front desk to housekeeping, we are here to serve you and ensure that you have a memorable stay.
